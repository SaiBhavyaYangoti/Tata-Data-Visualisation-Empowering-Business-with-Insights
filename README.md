# Tata-Data-Visualisation-Empowering-Business-with-Insights

This project was completed as part of a job simulation on Forage in collaboration with Tata Consultancy Services (TCS). The objective was to analyze business data and create interactive dashboards in Power BI to uncover trends, highlight key performance indicators, and provide actionable insights. The simulation also involved preparing stakeholder-focused questions to facilitate meaningful discussions with senior leadership.

Through this project, I gained hands-on experience in business intelligence, data storytelling, and visualization techniques. By translating raw data into clear visuals, the dashboards demonstrated how data-driven insights can support executives in effective decision-making. The project emphasized the importance of aligning analytics with business needs and showcasing results in a way that is both impactful and accessible.
