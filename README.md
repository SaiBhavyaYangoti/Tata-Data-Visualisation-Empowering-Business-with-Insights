# Tata Data Visualisation: Empowering Business with Insights

A business intelligence project completed as part of the **Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) Job Simulation** on Forage. The objective was to analyze online retail data and build interactive Power BI dashboards that surface key business insights for executive decision-making.

## Overview

Raw transactional data from an online retail business was analyzed to uncover revenue trends, market performance, customer behavior, and global product demand patterns. The project simulated a real-world BI workflow — from understanding business questions to delivering executive-ready visual stories.

## Dashboards Built

**1. Monthly Revenue Trend (2011)**
- Tracks total revenue across all months
- Highlights peak revenue of 168K in December
- Identifies seasonal patterns and low-performance months for strategic planning

**2. Top 10 Countries by Revenue and Quantity (Excluding UK)**
- Compares total revenue vs quantity sold across top international markets
- Key markets: EIRE, France, Netherlands, Germany, Norway
- Helps leadership prioritize high-value vs high-volume markets

**3. Top 10 Customers by Revenue**
- Identifies highest-value customers driving the most revenue
- Supports targeted retention and loyalty strategies

**4. Global Product Demand Map (Excluding UK)**
- Geographic visualization of product demand across countries
- Highlights untapped or emerging markets for expansion decisions

## Key Business Insights

- December recorded the highest monthly revenue at 168K, indicating strong seasonal demand
- EIRE is the top international market by both revenue and quantity
- A small segment of customers contributes disproportionately to total revenue — indicating high value customer concentration
- Several European markets show high revenue but lower quantity, suggesting premium product positioning

## Stakeholder Preparation

As part of the simulation, analytical questions were prepared for discussions with the CEO and CMO, covering:

- Which regions and time periods are driving the most revenue?
- Which customer segments should be prioritized for retention?
- What expansion opportunities exist in international markets?
- How can seasonal trends inform inventory and marketing decisions?
